Kamala Harris



    Policies:


  • Tax Reform: Proposes raising taxes on individuals earning over $400,000 annually while providing tax credits for first-time homebuyers.

  • Child Tax Credit Expansion: Plans to increase the child tax credit to $6,000 for families with newborns, aiming to support low- and middle-income households.

  • Affordable Housing: Intends to build 3 million new housing units and offer up to $25,000 in down-payment assistance for first-time homebuyers to address housing affordability.

  • Healthcare: Supports capping insulin prices at $35 for all Americans and expanding Medicare's ability to negotiate drug prices to reduce prescription costs.

  • Climate Change: Advocates for significant investments in clean energy and aims to achieve a zero-emissions economy by 2045

  • Immigration Reform: Supports comprehensive immigration reform, including pathways to citizenship for certain undocumented immigrants, and plans to hire additional border agents to enhance security.
  • Reproductive Rights: Committed to restoring and protecting abortion rights, making it a central theme of her campaign.

  • Criminal Justice Reform: Proposes ending mandatory minimum sentences, cash bail, and the death penalty, aiming to address systemic inequalities in the justice system.

  • Education: Plans to increase teacher salaries and invest in public education to improve educational outcomes nationwide.

  • Foreign Policy: Supports continued aid to Ukraine in its defense against Russian aggression and advocates for a nuanced approach to Israel's security and humanitarian concerns.

Donald Trump



    Policies:


  • Immigration Reform: Plans to carry out the largest domestic deportation operation in U.S. history, aiming to remove undocumented immigrants and implement stricter border controls.

  • Tax Policy: Proposes extending the 2017 Tax Cuts and Jobs Act, reducing the corporate tax rate to 15% for companies manufacturing in the U.S., and eliminating taxes on Social Security benefits

  • Trade Policy: : Intends to impose a universal baseline tariff on most foreign goods, with higher tariffs on Chinese imports, to encourage domestic manufacturing and reduce trade deficits.

  • Energy Policy: Aims to achieve energy independence by expanding fossil fuel production, rolling back regulations on oil and gas industries, and withdrawing from international climate agreements.

  • Healthcare: : Plans to repeal the Affordable Care Act and replace it with a system that emphasizes health savings accounts and state-level management of health insurance markets.

  • Education: Proposes eliminating the Department of Education, shifting control to state and local governments, and promoting school choice through vouchers and charter schools.
  • Criminal Justice: Advocates for stricter law enforcement policies, including deploying the National Guard to high-crime areas, imposing the death penalty for drug dealers, and implementing mandatory minimum sentences.

  • Foreign Policy: : Plans to reduce U.S. involvement in international alliances like NATO, adopt an "America First" approach, and reassess foreign aid commitments.

  • Social Policies: Seeks to roll back protections for transgender individuals, limit diversity and inclusion programs, and appoint conservative judges to influence social policy decisions.

  • Infrastructure: Proposes building "freedom cities" on federal land, investing in futuristic transportation like flying cars, and revitalizing urban areas through private-public partnerships.
